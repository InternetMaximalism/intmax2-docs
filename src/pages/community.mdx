## Contributing

We welcome contributions from developers, designers, researchers, and anyone passionate about privacy and scalability.

### Steps to Contribute

1. **Fork & clone** the repository on GitHub.
2. **Create a new branch** for your fix or feature.
3. **Commit your changes** with a clear message.
4. **Open a pull request**, following the PR template.

Please review our [Contribution Guidelines](https://github.com/InternetMaximalism/intmax2/blob/main/CONTRIBUTING.md) before submitting.
